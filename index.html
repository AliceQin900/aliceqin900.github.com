About Me

I received my B.S. degree in 2013 and will receive the Ph.D degree in 2018, from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University. Before graduation, I worked as an International Visiting Research Trainee in the lab of Information Retrieval and Knowledge Management in York University, Canada (2017.11-2018.02).

Research

My research interests include information retrieval, natural language processing, and deep learning applied to text data. I have publications at top conferences and journals like AAAI, SIGIR, TKDE and TIST.
